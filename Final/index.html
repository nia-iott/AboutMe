All About Me

Nia Iott

Hi, I am Nia Iott. I am 19 years old. I was born in Fort Wayne, Indiana. I have lived in Indiana my entire life. One of my dreams in life is to travel and get to see the world. I have only been to four state including the one I live in. I have been to Ohio, Illinois, and Michigan. The farthest north I have ever traveled was the UP of Michigan. My dream is to see the ocean and be able to taste it. I know it will taste bad but I want to say that I have done it.

I have a few goals in life. My first goal is to become a home owner before I turn the age of 21. I think it would be amazing to own my own house so young. I also have never been on an airplane and I think it sounds amazing. I plan to fly somewhere in the next year or so.
